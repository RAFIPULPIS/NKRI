History
Of Indonesia

Indonesia declared independence on August 17, 1945, gaining Dutch recognition in 1949. The New Order era (1966-1998) saw growth and issues under Suharto, followed by the Reformasi period with democratic reforms.

Independence from Colonial Rule (1945)

On August 17, 1945, Indonesia declared its independence from Dutch colonial rule. This marked the culmination of years of struggle and resistance by the Indonesian people against colonial powers, leading to the establishment of the Republic of Indonesia.
